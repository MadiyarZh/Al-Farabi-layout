Journal overview

The journal of socio-humanitarian research "Alpharabi" highlights actual and debatable problems of philosophical-political and spiritual-cognitive nature of modern socio-humanitarian knowledge.

Published since 2003 by the Institute of Philosophy, Political Science and Religious Studies of the Committee of Science of the Ministry of Education and Science of the Republic of Kazakhstan.
